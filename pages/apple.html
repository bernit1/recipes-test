Apple

Description

Domesticated tree and fruit of the rose family (Rosaceae), one of the most widely cultivated tree fruits.

Ingredients

An apple is naturally produced and doesn't require ingredients in the way a recipe does. Apples are a fruit that grows on trees. They are primarily composed of:

  • Water
  • Carbohydrates
  • Fructose
  • Sucrose
  • Glucose

Steps

  1. Planting a seed or a grafted seedling
  2. Nurturing it
  3. Waiting for it to mature
  4. Bear fruit
