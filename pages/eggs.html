Eggs

Description

Eggs are oval, rounded objects with hard shells produced by female birds and some reptiles, often containing a developing baby animal

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Butter or Oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper for seasoning

Steps

  1. Heat a pan with oil or butter over medium heat
  2. Crack the egg into the pan
  3. Season with salt and pepper
  4. Cook until the whites are set
  5. Flip for over easy, over medium, or over hard
